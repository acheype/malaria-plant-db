How to participate ?



Everybody who wants to participate is welcome. Please contact the administrators.

Data introduced in MpDBc are first registered in an Excel preformatted document. This document synthetizes published information on antimalarial medicinal plant uses, pharmacological tests, in different countries, regions and communities where malaria is endemic.

Data are obtained from journals, reviews, reports, books and proceedings from conferences, reporting the use of plants against malaria together with their antimalarial evaluation, through in vivo and/or in vitro tests/clinical tests, published all over the world.

Thus, ethnobotanical publications reporting antimalarial medicinal plant uses are welcomed for inclusion, as well as pharmacological ones dealing with antimalarial activity of plant extracts and of pure compounds isolated from medicinal plants.

Only publications reporting plant species with Latin binomials are included. Publications containing solely vernacular name of plant species are discarded.
