The database of medicinal plants against malaria

MpDBc has been designed to register and synthesize published information on antimalarial medicinal plants: their traditional use, in vitro / in vivo biological activity, and toxicity.

The database is not a substitute for published material, as only selected data from the publication has been retained.

MpDBc is a contribution that could serve as a decision-making tool in the search for natural substances with antimalarial activity, validation and integration of traditional pharmacopoeia in health care systems.
