MpDBc has been designed to register and synthesize published information on antimalarial medicinal plants: their traditional use, in vitro / in vivo biological activity and toxicity.

The database is not a substitute for published material as only selected data has been included.

MpDBc serves as a decision-making tool in the search for natural substances with antimalarial activity, validation and integration of traditional pharmacopoeia in health care systems.

In MpDBc following data can be found: complete reference of the publication, country of origin of the research, latin name of the plant, traditional antimalarial recipe, antimalarial evaluation results, toxicity and comments from expert compiler.
